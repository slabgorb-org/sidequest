**Scene 1 — Setup (0:00–0:45) | Slide 1: Title**

Open SideQuest to the main menu. Have the character creation screen ready to launch but not yet open.

Say: *"We're going to show you what happens when you let the dice decide."*

Launch character creation. Show the standard flow — point-buy mode, stats laid out neatly, sliders available. Don't interact with it.

Say: *"This is how character creation looks today. Safe, predictable, optimized."*

---

**Scene 2 — The Problem (0:45–1:30) | Slide 2: Problem**

Stay on the standard chargen screen.

Point to the stat grid. Say: *"When players control where numbers go, everyone ends up looking the same. High strength if you're a fighter. High intelligence if you're a mage. The interesting characters — the cowardly paladin, the surprisingly wise barbarian — never get made on purpose."*

*Fallback if screen is unavailable: Show Slide 2 (screenshot of standard chargen with stats highlighted).*

---

**Scene 3 — The Feature (1:30–3:30) | Slide 3: What We Built**

Navigate to character creation. Find the **Roll the Bones** toggle at the top of the attribute panel — it shows a d6 icon and the label "3d6 In Order."

Click the toggle. The panel background shifts to a darker, textured treatment. A counter appears: **Rerolls remaining: 2/2**.

Say: *"When you flip this on, the game takes over. Watch."*

Click **Generate Attributes**. Six dice rolls animate in sequence — Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma — each one locking in as it lands. Show a result like: STR 7, DEX 14, CON 11, INT 16, WIS 9, CHA 6.

Say: *"You got a high-intelligence weakling with terrible charisma. That's a character. Now watch what you can do with your budget."*

Click the reroll icon next to Charisma. The counter ticks down: **Rerolls remaining: 1/2**. A new value rolls in — say, CHA 12.

Attempt to reroll Strength. Counter ticks: **Rerolls remaining: 0/2**. STR rerolls to, say, 9.

Attempt a third reroll on any stat. The button is grayed out. The counter reads **0/2** in amber.

Say: *"Two rerolls. That's your budget. The rest of that character is what the dice decided."*

*Fallback if toggle doesn't appear: Go to Slide 3 and show the before/after screenshot comparison.*

---

**Scene 4 — Why This Approach (3:30–4:30) | Slide 4: Why This Approach**

No live interaction needed — stay on the locked-out chargen screen with the final stat block visible.

Say: *"This is a deliberate design. It's opt-in — you have to choose the harder mode. And the two-reroll safety valve means you won't generate a character who literally can't survive the first encounter. But past that? Those numbers are yours, and you build around them."*

Point to the reroll counter UI. Say: *"The counter is always visible. You always know where you stand. No surprises, except the ones the dice already gave you."*

---

**Scene 5 — Wrap (4:30–5:30) | Roadmap / Questions**

Return to the standard chargen with Roll the Bones toggled off.

Say: *"Standard mode is unchanged. This is strictly additive. Players who want control keep it. Players who want the old-school experience now have it."*

Transition to roadmap slide.
