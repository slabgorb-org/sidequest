## Overview

This is the inventory analogue of the ADR-143/144 'bind the ruleset, don't balance it' doctrine: if a genre binds WWN, the gear should BE WWN gear. Licensing reality: WWN is CC0 (verbatim-reproducible); CWN likely CC0 (verify); SWN/AWN free editions carry no open license (derive-only); Fate Core has no equipment economy at all. Epic brings inventory in line with each bound ruleset world-by-world and resolves the standalone mechanical defects. Coordinates with epic-113 (ADR-140 genre->world boundary hygiene) — SRD-sourcing naturally yields the correct genre-baseline + world-override placement.
