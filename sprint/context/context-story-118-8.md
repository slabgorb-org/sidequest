## Acceptance Criteria
- Auth-bypass fix: in handlers/fate_action.py the acting player is resolved as 'acting_player_id = getattr(msg, "player_id", "") or sd.player_id'. A client can send a FATE_ACTION with a non-empty spoofed player_id pointing at another seated player, and the handler then acts AS that PC — sealing their commit, spending their fate point, or invoking their aspect. Drive seat resolution from sd.player_id (server-authenticated, Cf-Access identity bound at connect) as the SOLE source; treat msg.player_id as a server-set OUTPUT annotation, never trusted inbound identity.
- Add a regression test: a FATE_ACTION whose msg.player_id != the socket's authenticated identity must NOT act as the spoofed seat (it acts as the authenticated PC, or is rejected loud).
- Latent injection (LOW, ADR-047): FateActionPayload.skill is stored on FateSealedCommit (fate_conflict.py) WITHOUT sanitize_player_text — unlike aspect_text/boost.text which sanitize with explicit ADR-047 citations. No current narrator hint interpolates commit.skill, so it is latent, but apply sanitize_player_text(payload.skill) at the seal site to match the defensive posture before any future hint references it.
