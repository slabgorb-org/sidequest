## Acceptance Criteria
_Defined by TEA (The Architect) during RED phase, 2026-06-15, from the story title + SM Assessment._

- **AC1 — FateConfig schema + binding.** A `FateConfig` model exists in `genre/models/rules.py` (`extra="forbid"`) carrying a per-genre skill list with default ratings (`skills: dict[str,int]`), `refresh`, optional starting-aspect templates (high concept + trouble), and a stunt catalog. `RulesConfig` gains a `fate: FateConfig | None` field, and `RulesConfig.ruleset_config()` returns it when `ruleset == "fate"`. A pack with `ruleset == "fate"` and no `fate` block fails loud (No Silent Fallbacks), mirroring the swn/cwn attribute_map validator.
- **AC2 — ChargenResources carries the facet.** `ChargenResources` (`chargen_contribution.py`) gains `fate_sheet: FateSheet | None = None` (additive; existing effort/spellcasting/system_strain unchanged).
- **AC3 — Seed builds a populated sheet.** `FateRulesetModule.seed_chargen_resources` returns a `ChargenResources` whose `fate_sheet` is a populated `FateSheet`: `skills` copied from `FateConfig.skills`, a high-concept + trouble aspect pair seeded, `refresh`/`fate_points` from config. The seed does not depend on d20 stats or a class (works with `stats={}`, `class_def=None`).
- **AC4 — Builder attaches the facet.** Building a `ruleset: fate` pack through the production `CharacterBuilder` attaches the seeded sheet onto `CreatureCore.fate_sheet`. A non-fate pack leaves `fate_sheet is None` (regression guard — additive, WN/native unaffected).
- **AC5 — OTEL lie-detector.** Seeding emits a `fate.chargen.seeded` span (skill count, aspect count, refresh) with a `SPAN_ROUTES` entry so the GM panel sees the engine seeded the sheet.
- **AC6 — Wiring (mandatory, no source-grep).** A synthetic `ruleset: fate` pack driven through the real builder yields a PC with a populated `FateSheet`, AND the bound module resolves to `FateRulesetModule` (so combat routes to `fate_conflict`, not native). Paired negative: a WN/native pack resolves to a non-fate module and gets no fate_sheet. OTEL/fixture-driven behavior, never a `read_text()` grep.

**Out of scope (do NOT pull in):** the interactive Fate chargen flow — player-authored aspects, skill-pyramid allocation, stunt selection — is story 121-7 (F4a2), design-gated by 121-6. F4a only needs the apply layer + a default/template seed path.
