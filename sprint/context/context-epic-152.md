## Overview
Completes the WWN combat de-nativization (epic-108, ADR-143 'Bind the Ruleset, Don't Balance It'). 108-3 stripped ALL native combat beats off WWN hp_depletion defs (cdef.beats == []); 108-8 synthesized only the 'attack' action (beat_filter.is_wn_action_beat == {'attack'}) while item-use rode its own intercept. cast_spell, brace, and break_contact were stripped but NEVER synthesized — at dispatch (dice.py:414) they miss the attack intercept, fall into the empty-cdef lookup, and raise 'unknown beat_id ... available: []' before any round runs. LIVE OUTAGE: in WWN combat today you can attack and use an item, but you CANNOT cast a spell, brace, or disengage — a real player-facing regression across caverns_and_claudes/elemental_harmony/heavy_metal/barsoom, red since 108-3 landed ~2026-06-15. Keith ruling (brainstorm 2026-06-20, CORRECTED after WWN SRD reading): do NOT shape native into the SRD. cast IS a real WWN action -> route it to the WWN spell spine (152-2). brace/break_contact were the native 106-2 reprisal-mitigation model (tier-delta HP mitigation + whole-attack prevent + a per-beat reprisal) — WWN has NONE of those; REMOVE that scaffolding and replace it with the genuine WWN defensive actions: Total Defense (+2 AC + Shock immunity) and Fighting Withdrawal/Run (avoid the free attack on flee), resolved by WWN AC math (152-1). The opponent attacks once on its initiative slot vs the defender's AC — there is no per-beat reprisal. test_106_2 is REWRITTEN to WWN semantics, not kept as a native RED spec. Cast RED spec: test_dice_path_spell_cast_102_2 + per-pack dispatch cast tests + test_wwn_scene_harness_fixture_proof.
