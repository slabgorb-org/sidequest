## Problem
CORRECTED 2026-06-20 (Keith: 'why are we shaping native into SRD?' + WWN SRD reading; design doc docs/superpowers/specs/2026-06-20-wn-full-action-set-design.md). The original framing (synthesize native brace/break_contact beats + restore 106-2 reprisal-mitigation) was the ADR-143 trap — it ports native mechanics into the WWN binding: BeatKind.brace base:1 flat-HP mitigation via resolve_tier_deltas; break_contact prevents the WHOLE opponent attack; a per-beat opponent reprisal for brace to mitigate. WWN has NONE of these. WWN SRD: combat is side-initiative (2.4.1); the opponent attacks ONCE on its own turn (no per-beat reprisal); defense is Armor Class (2.4.4). The two WWN defensive actions are Total Defense (Instant Action — give up your Main Action; +2 Melee & Ranged AC and immune to Shock until the start of your next turn) and Fighting Withdrawal (Main Action — disengage from an adjacent melee attacker so a following Run provokes NO free attack; does NOT cancel the opponent's own-turn attack). (2) ADD WWN Total Defense: a committed Total Defense raises the defender's Melee/Ranged AC by +2 and grants Shock immunity until their next turn, resolved by the EXISTING WWN AC math (the opponent's d20+hit checks the boosted AC). (3) ADD WWN Fighting Withdrawal + Run: wire the deferred 'move' action so disengage avoids the free attack on a flee per 2.4.4 (does NOT cancel the opponent turn). (4) REWRITE test_106_2_wwn_defensive_reprisal to WWN semantics (AC+2 / Shock-immunity / free-attack-on-flee), NOT tier-delta mitigation or attack-cancellation. Magnitudes are SRD-verbatim (+2 AC, Shock immunity) — never invented. Preserve 108-8's closed-allowlist + isinstance gate.
