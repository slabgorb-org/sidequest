## Technical Approach

**APPROVED by Architect (The Man in Black). Do not re-design or propose alternatives.**

The space_opera dogfight ship weapon `multifocal_laser` gets a dedicated genre-tier `ship_weapons` collection home.

**Rationale:** SWN starship weapons are ship-scale (3d4 damage), but native dogfight is personal-scale (opponent hp:8). Binding verbatim SWN weapons would either silently rebalance the duel or falsify provenance. Per SOUL "Bind the Ruleset, Don't Balance It," real SWN starship combat would replace native dogfight, not feed it one weapon. Solution: a bespoke `ship_weapons` collection at genre tier (legal per ADR-145 D3 because "native-subsystem config, not personal SRD gear") with inline weapon specs, resolved by dogfight only.

**Chosen option:** 2b — reuse existing CatalogItem model (not 2a inline specs, not verbatim SWN starship weapons).

**Implementation (server + content):**

**SERVER (sidequest-server):**
1. Add `ship_weapons: list[CatalogItem] = []` to `InventoryConfig` (sidequest/genre/models/inventory.py) — genre-tier-only, no per-world override in v1.
2. `resolve_inventory` (sidequest/server/dispatch/inventory_resolve.py) carries `ship_weapons` from genre straight through (genre-tier, NOT run through world item_catalog merge).
3. Dogfight `weapon_lookup` (built in sidequest/server/narration_apply.py, consumed by sidequest/game/dogfight_shot.py `_resolve_weapon`) resolves player_weapon/opponent_weapon ids against `ship_weapons` ONLY — reuse existing fail-loud ValueError on missing id.
4. `_validate_genre_baseline_no_bespoke` (sidequest/genre/loader.py) unchanged — scans item_catalog only, so bespoke ship weapons are exempt by design.
5. OTEL: annotate dogfight weapon-resolution span with source=ship_weapons, weapon id, and AP value (subsystem-decision visibility).

**CONTENT (sidequest-content):**
6. Add `ship_weapons:` block to genre_packs/space_opera/inventory.yaml with single `multifocal_laser` (preserve stats: damage 1d4 / armor_piercing 20 / category:weapon; provenance.mode: bespoke with comment crediting SWN-laser lineage, NOT provenance struct).
7. Delete triplicated `multifocal_laser` from genre_packs/space_opera/worlds/{aureate_span,coyote_star,perseus_cloud}/inventory.yaml (keep 2 world-distinct items per world).
8. Replace explanatory comment at space_opera/inventory.yaml:89-93 to point at new ship_weapons home.
