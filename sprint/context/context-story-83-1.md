## Problem
Deferred from ping-pong #74 (PR #638). That fix classifies a narrator creature mention (NpcMention.is_creature) and keeps the person namer off it, but a promoted creature Npc still carries NO bestiary identity: _promote_pool_member_to_npc builds a person-shaped Npc (placeholder HpPool 10/10, no creature_id/threat_level/abilities/morale) and only reports is_creature on telemetry. The Npc model ALREADY supports creature fields (creature_id/threat_level/abilities/morale, session.py); the Monster Manual generator exists (ADR-059; _creature_patch_from_enemy in dispatch/monster_manual_inject.py returns an NpcPatch with those fields). WIRE them: when a creature-typed pool member promotes (or at the creature mention seam), draw a bestiary/creature identity — species, threat profile, HP, abilities, morale — from the Monster Manual instead of a person placeholder, with NO culture/person-name. This is the 'route creatures to Monster Manual creature generation' half of the #74 fix direction. Per CLAUDE.md 'Don't Reinvent — Wire Up What Exists': the MM and the Npc creature fields exist; the wire from a narrator-classified creature to MM generation does not.

## Acceptance Criteria
- A creature-typed pool member (is_creature=True) promotes to an Npc carrying a Monster Manual creature identity: creature_id set, threat_level set, HP from the bestiary (not the 10/10 placeholder), abilities/morale populated where the MM provides them.
- The promoted creature Npc has NO culture and NO person-name — its name remains the narrator's descriptive name (or a bestiary species label), never a culture-corpus person-name.
- An OTEL span records the bestiary draw (creature_id, threat_level, hp, source) so the GM panel can verify the engine generated a creature identity rather than winging it. is_creature on the spawn span is True for these.
- Persons (is_creature=False) are completely unaffected — they still promote through the existing person path; no regression in the 72-x NPC-identity suite.
- A wiring test drives a creature mention end-to-end (mention → pool → promotion) and asserts the resulting Npc has MM creature fields populated and reachable from a production code path.
